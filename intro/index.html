Potori | Intro

Potori is a web application to gather and visualize your Ingress Portal nominations. Potori will obtain your mails related to Portal nominations from your Gmail inbox, so you must login with your account to use it.

Permission

Potori requires the following permissions:

Permission Describe
Gmail: Read Only
REQUIRED		 This is the core permission on which Potori is dependent. Potori will obtain mails related to Ingress Portal nomination, then extract and display informations from them.
The mail IDs will be saved in data file if you save the data file in your computer or your Google Drive. Except that, non of your mail data will be sent out of your browser page.
Google Drive: Files
RECOMMENDED		 Potori could upload your nomination data to your Google Drive manually, and download the data before fetching your mails.
By using the data file, Potori will not obtain and process all mails every time, which could reduce the network traffic and time useage.
The file will be storaged in a private app folder where only Potori can access.

Workflow

This part will describe how Potori works.
For those mails related to Portal nominations, the subjects and contents are generated from several templates, some keywords are used to filter mails and extract informations.

  1. Open or download the nomination data file
    If you have saved a nomination data file locally, you can open it to review your nominations without login.
    After login, Potori will try to download nomination data from your Google Drive if you have uploaded before.
  2. Obtain the target mails with filters
    Potori will obtain mails with the following filters:
    Mails Filters
    Confirmation from:ingress-support@nianticlabs.com Portal submission confirmation -edit -photo
    from:nominations@portals.ingress.com (有機會加入 OR 数週間 OR (few weeks))
    Acceptance from:ingress-support@nianticlabs.com Portal review complete now available -edit -photo
    from:nominations@portals.ingress.com Intel Map AP
    Rejection from:ingress-support@nianticlabs.com Portal review complete reviewed -edit -photo
    from:nominations@portals.ingress.com (完畢: OR 審査の完了 OR (REVIEW COMPLETE)) -AP

    Tips: You can also create rules with these filter to label your mails automatically in Gmail.
  3. Extract informations from the mails
    The contents will be used to extract information of the nomination.
    Content Useage
    Mail ID Avoid obtaining and processing the same mail every time, if you save or upload the data file.
    Subject Extract title of Portal.
    Timestamp Regarded as the confirmation / acceptance / rejection time of the nomination.
    Photo URL Show photo in the card.
    Generate a short UID as the ID of Portal and identify them in mails.
    [FULL VERSION ONLY] The short UID will be used to query Brainstorming database and generate link to Brainstorming watermeter.
    Intel Map URL Extract the coordinate and display a marker on the map.
    Mail Body Extract the rejection reason.

    As for the rejection mails, there are several templates for each rejection reason, and each one colud be identified by matching the keywords:
    Reason Filters
    Duplicated duplicate of either an existing Portal
    Too Close too close to an existing Portal
    能量塔過近
    Undeclared (Match neither of the keywords above)
  4. Save or upload the nomination data file
    You can save the nomination data to your computer by clicking the save button, a file named Potori.json will be downloaded.
    The nomination data will NOT be uploaded to your Google Drive automatically. If you have login, you can upload the data by clicking the cloud_upload button.

That's how Potori gets information of your Portal nominations from your inbox. Ingress Prime uses localized templates, which makes it difficult to find the corrent keywords, I'm working on it.

Useage

After login, the process will start automatically, there will be a progress bar to indicate the progress.
When it finishes, your nominations will be displayed as cards in the list and markers on the map.
You can click the equalizer button to open the Status & About dialog and filter the Portals by status and rejected reason.
